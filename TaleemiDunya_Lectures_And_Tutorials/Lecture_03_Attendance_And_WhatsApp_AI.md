### 📋 1. Marking Daily Student Attendance (`/school-admin/attendance`)
Every morning, classroom teachers or the receptionist can mark attendance within 30 seconds:
1. Open **`Left Sidebar -> Attendance -> Student Attendance`**.
2. Select your Class (`e.g., Class 10 - Section A`) and choose the Date (`default is Today`).
3. You will see a clean table with all active students listed. By default, everyone is marked **Present (`P - Green`)**.
4. If a student like `Zainab Ahmed` is absent, click the **`A (Red)`** button next to her name. If on medical leave, click **`L (Yellow)`**.
5. Click **`💾 Save Daily Attendance`**.

---

### 🤖 2. Instant WhatsApp Absentee Alert (`Magic Moment!`)
The exact second you click `Save Daily Attendance`, our background Node.js WhatsApp Bot (`running on Alwaysdata Cloud`) wakes up:
* It scans the attendance list for any student marked **`A (Absent)`**.
* It grabs the parent's phone number (`e.g., 0300-1234567`) and shoots an official WhatsApp message directly to the parent's mobile phone:
  > *🔔 **TaleemiDunya Attendance Alert***  
  > *Dear Parent, your child **Zainab Ahmed (Class 10)** has been marked **ABSENT** today (15-Jul-2026). Please contact school administration office immediately if there is any emergency.*

### ⏰ 3. Automated Morning Fee & Birthday Cron Jobs (`/school-admin/whatsapp-automation`)
You do not need to wake up early or remember to send monthly reminders! TaleemiDunya-Pro has built-in automated **Cron Clocks**:
* **🎂 Morning Birthday Wishes (`08:00 AM Daily`):** Every morning at 8:00 AM, the bot checks if any student or teacher has their birthday today. If yes, it sends a personalized happy birthday greeting with your school logo!
* **💰 Morning Fee Reminders (`10:00 AM Daily`):** Every morning at 10:00 AM, the bot checks which parents have unpaid fee challans past their due date and automatically sends a polite, professional reminder via WhatsApp!

---

### 🛡️ 4. AI Chatbot Self-Healing & QR Scan Setup (`/school-admin/whatsapp-bot`)
If you ever change your school phone number or disconnect WhatsApp Web:
1. Go to **`Left Sidebar -> WhatsApp Automation -> WhatsApp AI Bot`**.
2. Click **`🔄 Generate New QR Code`**.
3. Open WhatsApp on your school smartphone -> tap **Linked Devices -> Link a Device -> Scan the QR Code on screen**.
4. Once scanned, the bot displays **`🟢 CONNECTED & ONLINE`** and runs autonomously 24 hours a day, 7 days a week!
