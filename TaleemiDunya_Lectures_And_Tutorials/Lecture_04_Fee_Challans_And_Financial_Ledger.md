### 🧾 1. Monthly Fee Challan Generator (`/school-admin/challan-book`)
Gone are the days of writing fee books by hand! You can generate professional bank-ready fee challans for hundreds of students in seconds:
1. Open **`Left Sidebar -> Accounts & Finance -> Challan Book`**.
2. **Set Your Billing Configuration:**
   * Enter Billing Month (`e.g., October 2026`) and Due Date (`e.g., 10th October`).
   * Enter Bank Details (`e.g., Allied Bank Limited, Account # 01-100-3942-01`).
3. **Configure Fee Structure (`Add Fee Items`):**
   * Add dynamic items like `Tuition Fee (Rs. 2500)`, `Generator Fund (Rs. 200)`, `Computer Lab Fee (Rs. 500)`.
4. Click **`🖨️ Print Batch Challans`**:
   * The software automatically creates **3-Part Bank Challan Vouchers (`Parent Copy, School Copy, Bank Copy`)** complete with barcodes, student details, and automatically added previous arrears!

### 🚨 2. Fee Defaulters & Legal Notice Engine (`/school-admin/fee-defaulters`)
When parents delay fee payments for multiple months, our automated Defaulter Hub detects them immediately:
1. Open **`Left Sidebar -> Accounts & Finance -> Fee Defaulters`**.
2. Notice the intelligent categorization:
   * **🟡 Overdue:** Students whose fee is delayed (`Total Due < Rs. 10,000`).
   * **🔴 Critical Defaulters:** Students whose unpaid dues exceed **`Rs. 10,000`** across multiple months (`May, June, July`).
3. **1-Click WhatsApp Defaulter Notice:**
   * Click the green **`📲 WhatsApp Notice`** button next to any defaulter.
   * The system sends an urgent financial reminder with exact breakdowns (`Total Due: Rs. 12,000 | Pending Months: June, July`) straight to the parent's WhatsApp!

---

### 💼 3. Payroll & Teacher Salary Disbursement (`/school-admin/payroll`)
Manage all your teachers and staff salaries transparently:
1. Open **`Left Sidebar -> Accounts & Finance -> Payroll Manager`**.
2. Look up your staff members (`e.g., Senior Math Teacher basic pay Rs. 65,000`).
3. The system automatically checks how many days the teacher was absent during the month (`e.g., 1 Absent = Rs. 2,500 deduction`) and adds any advance salary taken (`Advance Taken = Rs. 5,000`).
4. Click **`🖨️ Salary Slip`** to print a clean employee pay slip or click **`Toggle Paid`** to mark the month cleared!

---

### 📉 4. Daily Expense Ledger (`/school-admin/expenses`)
To know your exact monthly net profit (`Profit = Total Fee Collected - Total Expenses`), record daily expenditures:
* Open **`Accounts & Finance -> Expense Manager`**.
* Add expenses like `Electric Bill June 2026 (Rs. 18,500)`, `Whiteboard Markers (Rs. 3,200)`, `Generator Diesel (Rs. 4,500)`.
* Click **`🖨️ Petty Cash Voucher`** whenever you hand out cash to staff for auditing!
