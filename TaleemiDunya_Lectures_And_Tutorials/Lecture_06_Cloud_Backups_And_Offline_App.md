### 📶 2. Working Offline During Internet Outages (`PWA Offline Shield`)
What happens if the school Wi-Fi disconnects or load shedding cuts the internet while teachers are taking attendance?
* **Nothing Stops Working!** Look at the top navigation bar. When internet drops, the status changes to: **`🟡 OFFLINE MODE — Changes saved to device cache`**.
* Teachers can continue marking attendance, adding fee challans, and taking notes. The software saves everything into the device's internal **IndexedDB local storage**.
* The exact second Wi-Fi reconnects, the status turns **`🟢 ONLINE`** and all offline entries automatically sync back to cloud servers!
