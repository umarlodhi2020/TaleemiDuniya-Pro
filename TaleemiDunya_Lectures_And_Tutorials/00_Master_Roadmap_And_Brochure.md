### 📌 What is TaleemiDunya-Pro? (`Yeh Software Kya Hai Aur Kya Kaam Karta Hai?`)
**TaleemiDunya-Pro v2.5.0** is an Enterprise-Grade, 100% Cloud & Offline-Capable School Management ERP software designed specifically for modern Pakistani and international schools, academies, and college systems. 

Unlike traditional desktop software or slow web portals, TaleemiDunya-Pro integrates **4 world-class cloud technologies**:
1. **🔥 Google Firebase Firestore:** High-speed real-time NoSQL database with multi-tenant data isolation.
2. **☁️ Google Drive Double-Safe REST API Vault:** Automated 1-Click cloud backups saved straight to the owner's personal Google Drive folder (`/TaleemiDunya_Backups/`).
3. **🤖 Alwaysdata & PM2 Node.js 24/7 WhatsApp AI Bot:** Autonomous 24/7 WhatsApp messaging engine that sends fee challans, absentee alerts, morning reminders, and answers parent queries instantly.
4. **📱 Progressive Web App (PWA) + IndexedDB Offline Shield:** Allows schools to operate locally during internet power outages and auto-syncs when reconnected.

### 🗺️ Master Feature Matrix & Directory (`Kis Module Ka Kya Kaam Hai?`)

#### 🎓 1. Student Directory & Admissions Hub (`/school-admin/students`)
* **What it does:** Complete lifecycle management of students from admission to graduation.
* **Key Superpowers:**
  * **New Admission Engine:** Capture full student details, B-Form / CNIC, Guardian info, and upload live compressed student photos via Cloudinary CDN.
  * **Digital Student ID Card & Barcode Generator (`/school-admin/id-cards`):** Auto-generate printable student ID cards with custom school branding, validity period, barcodes, and biometric gate scanner support.
  * **Bulk Promotion Engine (`/school-admin/promote-students`):** Promote entire classes (`e.g., Class 9 -> Class 10`) at the end of the academic year with 1 click.

#### 📋 2. Attendance & WhatsApp AI Hub (`/school-admin/attendance`)
* **What it does:** Daily digital attendance tracking for both students and staff with live automated alerts.
* **Key Superpowers:**
  * **1-Click Class Attendance:** Mark Present (`P`), Absent (`A`), or Leave (`L`) quickly.
  * **Instant Absentee WhatsApp & SMS Alert Engine:** As soon as a student is marked Absent, an automated WhatsApp alert (`🤖 Taleemi AI`) instantly hits the parent's phone.
  * **Staff & Teacher Attendance (`/school-admin/staff-attendance`):** Track employee check-ins, check-outs, and monthly absence deductions automatically.
  * **Leave Requests Manager (`/school-admin/leaves`):** Approve or reject student/staff medical or casual leave applications online.

#### 💰 3. Financial Ledger & Fee Management Hub (`/school-admin/fee-challans`)
* **What it does:** Complete school financial control, tuition fee collection, and salary disbursement.
* **Key Superpowers:**
  * **Automated Monthly Challan Generator (`/school-admin/challan-book`):** Generate printable multi-part fee vouchers with tuition fees, transport charges, lab dues, and previous arrears calculated automatically.
  * **Defaulters Tracker & Notice Engine (`/school-admin/fee-defaulters`):** Identify overdue accounts (`> Rs. 10,000`) and send 1-click legal/polite fee reminder notices via WhatsApp.
  * **Payroll & Salary Manager (`/school-admin/payroll`):** Calculate teacher salaries with automatic deductions for absences and staff cash advances.
  * **Expense Ledger & Profit/Loss Sheet (`/school-admin/expenses`):** Track daily utility bills, generator fuel, maintenance, and stationery to monitor net monthly profit.

#### 📚 4. Academic Operations & Diary Hub (`/school-admin/academics`)
* **What it does:** Day-to-day curriculum management, timetable scheduling, and examinations.
* **Key Superpowers:**
  * **Daily Homework Diary (`/school-admin/daily-diary`):** Post daily homework for Math, Science, English, and Urdu that reflects instantly on the Parent & Student Mobile App.
  * **Class Timetable Builder (`/school-admin/timetable-builder`):** Build conflict-free period-by-period class schedules for teachers and rooms.
  * **Exam Results & Report Card Generator (`/school-admin/exams`):** Input subject marks and print professional school-branded student marksheets and grade cards.
  * **Online Quiz & Test Engine (`/school-admin/quiz-engine`):** Conduct multiple-choice computer-based quizzes with instant scoring.

#### 🏢 5. Facilities & Security Hub (`/school-admin/resources`)
* **What it does:** Manage school transport, library, hostel rooms, visitor logs, and gate security.
* **Key Superpowers:**
  * **Biometric Gate Pass Scanner (`/school-admin/gate-scanner`):** Scan student ID cards at the school entrance/exit to record exact entry/departure timestamps.
  * **Visitor Gate Pass Manager (`/school-admin/gate-passes`):** Issue digital early-exit passes for parents taking students for medical appointments or emergencies.
  * **Bus & Transport Fleet Manager (`/school-admin/transport`):** Track school buses, van routes, driver contacts, and monthly transport fees.
  * **Digital Library & Hostel Dorms (`/school-admin/library` & `/school-admin/hostel`):** Track book issuances and student dormitory allocations.

#### 🤖 6. AI Automations & Cloud Security (`/school-admin/whatsapp-automation`)
* **What it does:** Autonomous 24/7 background tasks and foolproof data protection.
* **Key Superpowers:**
  * **Morning Fee Reminders Cron (`10:00 AM Daily`):** Automatically shoots fee reminder alerts to pending parents every morning.
  * **Birthday Wishes Generator (`08:00 AM Daily`):** Sends personalized school-branded happy birthday wishes to students and staff.
  * **Double-Safe Google Drive Vault (`/school-admin/google-drive-backup`):** 1-Click cloud backup straight to `/TaleemiDunya_Backups/` folder on Google Drive (`Folder ID: 1EQPALkEACZkA9QajQXGGMl2ogehUIwbx`).
